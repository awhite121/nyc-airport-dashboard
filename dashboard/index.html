Known limitations

  • One month of historical yellow-taxi trips.
  • No live traffic, weather, incidents, road closures, or event data.
  • Only Manhattan pickups and JFK/LGA drop-offs.
  • Historical taxi-zone distance is a proxy before a live route exists.
  • Late risk is relative to typical conditions for an airport, hour, and weekday—not missed-flight probability.
